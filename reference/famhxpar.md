# famhxpar: Family History - Parents

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(famhxpar)
```

## Format

A data frame with 5907 rows and 11 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- MOTHER: Did/Does the biological mother have dementia or significant
  memory impairment?

- MOTHAGE: At what approximate age did symptoms start?

- MOTHDIAG: Did this relative receive a diagnosis of Alzheimer's
  disease?

- MOTHAUT: Was diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease confirmed by autopsy?

- FATHER: Did/Does the biological father have dementia or significant
  memory impairment?

- FATHAGE: At what approximate age did symptoms start?

- FATHDIAG: Did this relative receive a diagnosis of Alzheimer's
  disease?

- FATHAUT: Was diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease confirmed by autopsy?
