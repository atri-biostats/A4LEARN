# ruib: Resource Use Inventory - Brief

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(ruib)
```

## Format

A data frame with 9148 rows and 18 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- DONE: Was assessment/procedure done?

- NDREASON: If No, reason not done:

- RUIBCOMP: Form completed by:

- BRADMIT: 1. In the past one year, how many times were you admitted to
  the hospital for an overnight stay?

- BREXAM: 2. In the past 3 months, how many times have you been examined
  by a health care professional (not including study related
  examinations)?

- BRHELP: Paid help:

- BRUPHELP: Un-paid help:

- VOLUNTEER: 4a. Do you do volunteer work?

- VOLHRS: If yes, how many hours per week

- EMPLOY: 4b. Are you engaged in paid employment?

- EMPHRS: If yes, how many hours per week

- LOST: 4c. In the past 3 months, have you lost time in volunteer work
  or paid employment due to health reasons?

- LOSTHRS: If yes, how many hours per week

- RESIDENCE: 5. Have you changed your residence in the past year?

- RESTYPE: If Yes, please select the best description of your new
  residence:
