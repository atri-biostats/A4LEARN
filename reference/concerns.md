# concerns: Concerns about Alzheimer's Disease Dementia

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(concerns)
```

## Format

A data frame with 15471 rows and 12 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- DONE: Was assessment/procedure done?

- NDREASON: If No, reason not done:

- CMETHOD: Method of Collection:

- CADDVLP: 1. I am concerned that I will develop Alzheimer's disease
  dementia.

- CADDVLP5: 2. I am concerned that I will develop Alzheimer's disease
  dementia in the next 5 years.

- CADKNOW: 3. I would like to know if I am going to develop Alzheimer's
  disease dementia at some point later in my life.

- CADBLIEV: 4. I believe that I will someday develop Alzheimer's disease
  dementia.

- CADWRST: 5. Alzheimer's disease dementia is the worst disease I can
  think of.

- CADCNCRN: 6. My concern about developing Alzheimer's disease dementia
  is greater than my concern about other medical problems.
