# spinfo: Study Partner Information

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(spinfo)
```

## Format

A data frame with 7646 rows and 12 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- RECNO: NA

- BPID: Study Partner ID Number:

- INFRELAT: Relationship of the study partner to the participant

- INFGENDER: Gender of study partner

- INFAGE: Is study partner 90 or older?

- INFAGESP: If No, age of study partner:

- INFLIVE: Do the study partner and participant live together?

- INFHRS: How many hours per week does the study partner spend with the
  participant in-person?

- INFHRSOT: Other than in-person, how many hours per week does the study
  partner spend with the participant?
