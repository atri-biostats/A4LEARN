# rss: Research Satisfaction Survey

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(rss)
```

## Format

A data frame with 7430 rows and 17 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- DONE: Was assessment/procedure done?

- NDREASON: If No, reason not done:

- RSSCOMP: Survey completed by:

- RSSQUAL: 1. How would you rate the quality of the tests and attention
  you have received during your participation in the study?

- RSSEXPECT: 2. To what extent has our research program met your
  expectations?

- RSSRECOM: 3. If a friend were to inquire about this research program,
  would you recommend it to him or her?

- RSSREDO: 4. If you had a chance to redo your decision to participate
  in this research program, do you think you would choose to
  participate?

- RSSBEST: 5. What do you like best about the study?

- RSSLEAST: 6. What do you like least?

- RSSMED: Study medication:

- RSSTST: Tests/Procedures:

- RSSVIS: Number of visits:

- RSSOTH: Other:

- RSSIPAD: 8. How would you rate the quality of the iPad Testing?
