# cfisp: Cognitive Function Index - Study Partner

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(cfisp)
```

## Format

A data frame with 15539 rows and 23 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- DONE: Was assessment/procedure done?

- NDREASON: If No, reason not done:

- BPID: Study Partner ID:

- SPMETHOD: Method of Collection:

- SPMEMORY: 1. Do you feel that the participant has had a significant
  decline in memory compared to one year ago?

- SPREPEAT: 2. Does the participant tend to ask the same question over
  and over?

- SPMISPLA: 3. Has the participant been misplacing things more often?

- SPWRITTN: 4. Compared to one year ago, does it seem to you that the
  participant is relying more on written reminders (e.g., shopping
  lists, calendars)?

- SPHELP: 5. Does the participant need more help from others to remember
  appointments, family occasions or holidays?

- SPRECALL: 6. Does the participant have more trouble recalling names,
  finding the right word, or completing sentences?

- SPDRIVE: 7. Is the participant having more trouble driving (e.g.,
  driving too slowly or too fast, having more trouble driving at night,
  getting lost, having accidents)?

- SPMONEY: 8. Compared to one year ago, is the participant having more
  difficulty managing money (e.g., paying bills, calculating change,
  completing tax forms)?

- SPSOCIAL: 9. Is the participant less interested in social activities?

- SPWORK: 10. Do you believe, based on your own observations or comments
  from the participant's co-workers, that the participant's work
  performance (paid or volunteer) has declined significantly, compared
  to one year ago?

- SPFOLLOW: 11. Does the participant have more trouble following the
  news or the plots of books, movies, or TV shows?

- SPACTIV: 12. Are there any activities (e.g., hobbies such as card
  games, crafts) that are substantially more difficult for the
  participant now compared to one year ago?

- SPLOST: 13. Is the participant more likely to become disoriented, or
  get lost, for example when traveling to another city?

- SPAPPLIA: 14. Compared to one year ago, does the participant have more
  difficulty using household appliances (such as washing machine or
  microwave) or electronic devices (such as computer or cell phone)?

- SPCONCERN: 15. In the past year, has the participant seen a doctor
  about memory concerns?

- CFSPTTOTAL: Total Score
