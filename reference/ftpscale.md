# ftpscale: Future Time Perspective Scale

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(ftpscale)
```

## Format

A data frame with 17664 rows and 16 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- DONE: Was assessment/procedure done?

- NDREASON: If No, reason not done:

- FTMETHOD: Method of Collection:

- FTOPPS: 1. Many opportunities await me in the future.

- FTGOAL: 2. I expect that I will set many new goals in the future.

- FTPOSSBL: 3. My future is filled with possibilities.

- FTLIFE: 4. Most of my life lies ahead of me.

- FTINFINIT: 5. My future seems infinite to me.

- FTANYTHNG: 6. I could do anything I want in the future.

- FTNEW: 7. There is plenty of time left in my life to make new plans.

- FTRUNOUT: 8. I have the sense that time is running out.

- FTLIMIT: 9. There are only limited possibilities in my future.

- FTGETOLD: 10. As I get older, I begin to experience time as limited.
