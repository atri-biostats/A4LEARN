# famhxsib: Family History - Sibling Log

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(famhxsib)
```

## Format

A data frame with 14600 rows and 11 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- RECNO: NA

- SIBYOB: Biological sibling year of birth:

- SIBMOTHER: Specify relationship:

- SIBFATHER: NA

- SIBDEMENT: Did/Does this sibling have dementia or significant memory
  impairment?

- SIBAGE: At what approximate age did symptoms start?

- SIBDIAG: Did this relative receive a diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease?

- SIBAUT: Was diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease confirmed by autopsy?
