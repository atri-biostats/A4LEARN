# views: Views & Perceptions of Amyloid Imaging

ATRI clinical analysis dataset.

## Usage

``` r
data(views)
```

## Format

A data frame with 10296 rows and 16 variables.

## Details

- SUBSTUDY: NA

- BID: NA

- VISCODE: NA

- DONE: Was assessment/procedure done?

- NDREASON: If No, reason not done:

- VMETHOD: Method of Collection:

- VSEEK: 1. To seek information on preventative measures (e.g., change
  diet, exercise, or other lifestyle changes)

- VEASE: 2. To put my mind at ease if I found out I do not have elevated
  amyloid on my PET scan.

- VRISK: 3. To know more about my risk of developing Alzheimer's disease
  dementia.

- VPART: 4. To be able to participate in anti-amyloid clinical trials
  (such as the A4 trial)

- VCNTRB: 5. The desire to contribute to research on Alzheimer's disease

- VAFFAIR: 6. To arrange my personal affairs

- VCNFRM: 7. To confirm the feeling that I might already be developing
  symptoms of Alzheimer's disease dementia

- VPREP: 8. To prepare my family for my possible illness in the future

- VCURIOUS: 9. Curiosity

- VOTHER: 10. Other (please list)
